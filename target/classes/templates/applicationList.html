Frequently Asked Questions

The Education Scholarship is available for children, spouses, and siblings of service members who are pursuing professional courses.

The scholarship covers tuition fees plus Rs 5,000/- for books, subject to a maximum of Rs 25,000/- per annum.

The Penury Grant is provided for life time, effective from 01 Apr 2017, at the rate of Rs. 4000/- per month.

Our Aim

Our goal is to support those suffering from the loss of a loved one due to battle casualties. This assistance is crucial for bereaved families, addressing financial, educational, medical needs, and encouraging volunteer work.
