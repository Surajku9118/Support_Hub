Application Process

The application process is straightforward and can be completed online. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Review the requirements: Make sure you meet the necessary qualifications and have the required documents.
  2. Fill out the application form: Click the link below to access our online application form. It should take about 10-15 minutes to complete.
  3. Submit your application: Once you've completed the form, click submit. We'll review your application and be in touch within 3-5 business days.
  4. Attend an orientation: If your application is successful, we'll invite you to an orientation session where you'll learn more about our organization and the volunteer roles available.
Learn more about the requirements

Requirements

To volunteer with us, you'll need to meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have a valid government-issued ID
  • Provide proof of vaccination (where applicable)
  • Pass a background check (where applicable)
